My Interests

The beautiful connections between mathematics and physics, which are established by many of the most significant physical theories, have always attracted me. Below you can find a summary of my current field of work or subjects I would like to investigate further.

Mathematics

Currently, I am trying to absorb the vast amount of concepts representing the cornerstones of Algebraic Topology. Further, I am interested in Riemannian Geometry in order to better understand General Relativity. The abstract ideas behind Category Theory are also very fascinating, particularly with regard to the process of Quantization and its abstract iteration over the empty set, leading to nth Quantization. Nice!

Physics

The conceptual differences between Quantum Mechanics and General Relativity have already puzzled me during my physics studies. The merging of these two theories is considered as the holy grail among physicists and the quest for it is very exciting. Loop Quantum Gravity, String Theory and other exotic theories try to solve the puzzle. I also find Classical Mechanics and its formulation via Symplectic Geometry quite attractive and should spend more time on it.
