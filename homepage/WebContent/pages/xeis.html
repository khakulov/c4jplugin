XEIS - X Elastic Interaction Simulation

XEIS

This is a project I worked on during my ERASMUS year in Grenoble. It was related to a university project which kind of marked the end of the year. The programm is written in C++ with the ROOT toolkit from CERN and allows the visualization of elastic defect-defect interactions on a lattice.
